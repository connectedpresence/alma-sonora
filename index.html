Who we are

Karina & Juan

Founder · Sound therapist

Karina Ríos

Karina came to sound therapy after more than 27 years accompanying women through processes of transformation. Certified by the Instituto de Sonoterapia Española under the guidance of Mariela Ortiz, she holds spaces where sound becomes gentle medicine and presence becomes a bridge toward healing.

Co-facilitator · Holistic therapist

Juan Saer

Juan came to healing after a long inner road — the searching, the loss, and the return to sacred silence. Certified in the CHIVU DAY HAI method under the guidance of Mariela Ortiz, he integrates sound therapy, conscious breathwork, bodywork, and compassionate inquiry, and gives himself fully through the deep sound of his gong.

Together they hold a dance between the feminine and the masculine that doesn't settle on one side — at times one opens what the other cradles, at times one guides while the other holds. One sustained listening, held by two bodies.
